About us

Welcome to DuckMC! We’re an exciting Minecraft minigame server featuring a variety of unique and fun minigames. Our server is a great place for players of all ages to come together and explore the world of Minecraft.

At DuckMC, we strive to provide an enjoyable experience for everyone. We have an extensive selection of minigames, ranging from classic ones like TNT Run and Skywars to new ones like Dragon Defense and Capture the Flag. We also offer special events like tournaments and giveaways to keep things interesting.

We take great pride in our server’s performance. We have a dedicated team of moderators who are always on hand to ensure that DuckMC provides a safe, fair, and fun environment for everyone. Our technical team is always working to make sure the server runs smoothly and efficiently.

We also put a lot of effort into making sure our community is enjoyable. We have a thriving forum where players can discuss minigames, share tips and tricks, and generally have a good time. We also have a Discord server for players to chat with each other.

We hope you’ll join us at DuckMC and enjoy all the unique minigames and exciting events that we offer. Thank you for considering us, and we look forward to seeing you on the server!
